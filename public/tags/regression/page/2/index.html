Friday in the Plenary

July 7, 2017 Jackson

4 minute read

R Consortium sponsor talk The R Consortium is a not-for-profit organisation which funds most of the key infrastructure for the R Project. Sponsors include IBM, Microsoft, Google, RStudio as well as several tech firms and data consultancies. They exist as a group of business enterprises interested in R thriving in the long-term. In essence a way for businesses who use R to contribute back. They see themselves as the link between the R Foundation, Industry and the R Community.

Thursday In The Plenary

July 7, 2017 Jackson

4 minute read

Open Analytics sponsor talk Open Analytics is a consulting group based in Belgium who call themselves open source fundamentalists. The talk went through 2 examples of the kind of projects they do. The first was about parasite worms in Africa. The second was to find out Dutch preferences for different flavours of soup. Turns out Dutch women like Mexican soup, whereas the men don’t mind too much about the flavour.
